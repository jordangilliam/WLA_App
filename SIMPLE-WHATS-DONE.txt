TO DEPLOY (10 MINUTES)
-----------------------
1. Go to vercel.com
2. Sign in with GitHub
3. Import: jordangilliam/WLA_App
4. Add environment variables:
   (Copy from your .env.local file)
   - NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL
   - NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY
   - SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY
   - NEXTAUTH_SECRET
   - NEXTAUTH_URL (will be given by Vercel)
   - GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID
   - GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET
   - AZURE_AD_CLIENT_ID
   - AZURE_AD_CLIENT_SECRET
   - AZURE_AD_TENANT_ID
   - NEXT_PUBLIC_MAPBOX_TOKEN
5. Click "Deploy"
6. Wait 3 minutes
7. YOU HAVE A LIVE URL!

Then:
8. Update NEXTAUTH_URL in Vercel to your live URL
9. Add redirect URIs to Google/Azure OAuth apps
10. SHARE WITH WLA!
