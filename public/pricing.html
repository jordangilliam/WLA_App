Frequently Asked Questions

Can we try before we buy?
Absolutely! All paid tiers include a 30-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card required to start.
What if we grow beyond our license?
You can upgrade anytime. We'll pro-rate your current subscription and only charge the difference. No penalties for growth!
Is our data secure and FERPA compliant?
Yes! We're fully FERPA, COPPA, and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell student data. Full compliance documentation available.
Can we self-host the platform?
The open-source core can be self-hosted on your own servers. Enterprise customers can also deploy on-premise with our support (additional fees apply).
What about training and implementation?
School tier includes online training. District+ includes live workshops. Enterprise includes unlimited training and dedicated implementation support. All tiers get comprehensive documentation.
Can we customize the platform?
District tier allows custom content and branding. Enterprise tier includes custom feature development. All customizations are scoped and priced individually.
What happens if we cancel?
You have 30 days to export all your data in standard formats (CSV, JSON). After that, data is securely deleted. No lock-in, ever.
Do you offer multi-year discounts?
Yes! Pay for 2 years upfront and get 10% off. Pay for 3 years and get 15% off. Great for budget planning.
