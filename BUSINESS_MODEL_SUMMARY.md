### School Tier
**Price**: $1,500/year  
**Users**: Up to 100 students

**Includes**:
- Everything in Free tier
- Managed hosting
- Admin dashboard
- Basic analytics
- Email support (48hr)
- Automatic updates
- Data export

**Target Audience**:
- Individual schools
- Small conservation programs
- Youth organizations

**Discounts**:
- Non-profit: 20% off ($1,200/year)
- Title I: 30% off ($1,050/year)
- Rural/underserved: 40% off ($900/year)

- School districts

- Phone support
- Unlimited training
- Co-branding opportunities

**Target Audience**:
- Statewide conservation programs
